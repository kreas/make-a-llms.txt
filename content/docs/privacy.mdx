**Last updated: 27 May 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Ready ("AI Ready", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by Hunt Gather Create {/* [CONFIRM: legal entity name and registration] */}, collects, uses, and shares information when you use the website at [aiready.cat](https://www.aiready.cat) and the AI Ready platform (the "Service").

AI Ready helps website owners make their sites readable and citable by AI systems. To do this, the Service crawls URLs you submit, processes page content, and sends that content to third-party AI providers for analysis. This policy describes what that means for your data and the data of the sites you analyze.

## Who this policy covers

This policy applies to two groups:

- **Account holders** who sign up for and use the Service.
- **Website data** that account holders submit for analysis, including page content, robots.txt files, sitemaps, and access logs.

If you submit a website for analysis, you confirm that you are authorized to do so and that your use of the Service complies with the policies of any third party whose content you submit.

## Information we collect

## Information you provide

When you create an account or use the Service, we collect:

- Account details such as name, email address, and password credentials.
- Billing information processed through our payment provider {/* [CONFIRM: payment processor, e.g. Stripe] */}. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.
- The URLs, domains, and websites you submit for analysis.
- Any content you upload, such as access logs or sitemap files.
- Communications you send to us, including support requests and feedback.

### Information collected automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect:

- Usage data such as features accessed, audits run, and tests performed.
- Device and connection data such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.
- Cookies and similar technologies as described in the Cookies section below.

### Information collected from analyzed websites

To produce audits, summaries, scores, and tests, the Service fetches and processes content from the URLs you submit. This may include page text, headings, metadata, structured data (schema markup), robots.txt directives, sitemaps, and, where you provide them, server access logs containing crawler user agents and request data.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including generating llms.txt files, per-page markdown, summaries, audits, citation-readiness scores, and provider tests.
- Send content from analyzed pages to third-party AI providers for comprehension, extraction, citation, and hallucination testing.
- Run scheduled jobs such as weekly citation tracking and trend calculation.
- Manage your account, process payments, and provide customer support.
- Improve and develop the Service, including debugging and analytics.
- Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal information. {/* [CONFIRM] */}

## AI providers and sub-processors

A core function of the Service is sending page content to third-party AI providers to test how those systems understand and cite your content. When you run a test or generate a summary, content from the relevant pages is transmitted to one or more of the following providers {/* [CONFIRM: verify the exact list of providers in production] */}:

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Google (Gemini)
- Perplexity

Each provider processes the data under its own terms and privacy practices. We send only the content needed to perform the requested operation. We rely on the Vercel AI SDK to route requests across providers.

We also use service providers for hosting, storage, payment processing, and analytics. A current list of sub-processors is available on request {/* [CONFIRM: consider publishing a sub-processor list and linking it here] */}.

## How we share information

We share information only:

- With the AI providers and sub-processors described above, to deliver the Service.
- With service providers acting on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.
- To comply with law, legal process, or enforceable governmental requests.
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of AI Ready, our users, or others.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.
